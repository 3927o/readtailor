**Kicker** — the editorial column-head that labels a section. Mono, uppercase, wide-tracked, with a leading green rule. Place directly above a serif headline.

```jsx
<Kicker>问题 · The Problem</Kicker>
<h2 className="headline">你买的书，大多没读完。</h2>
```

Use the bilingual `中文 · English` form — it's the brand's signature. Pass `center` for centered layouts. Keep it tiny (10px) and tracked; never bold it or remove the rule.
